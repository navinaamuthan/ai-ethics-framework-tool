## Social Impact
The proposed deployment of the COMPAS risk assessment tool poses significant ethical risks, including discrimination, privacy breaches, and potential physical harm to defendants. The tool's proprietary algorithm and lack of transparency raise concerns about accountability and the potential for biased decision-making. The historical precedent of COMPAS's racial bias and the risk of perpetuating existing social inequalities are also major concerns. Furthermore, the tool's use in informing detention and release decisions may lead to liberty violations and reputational harm.
