## Project Summary
This research proposes to analyse anonymised Dublin Bus and Luas smart card tap-on data to model public transport usage patterns across different times of day, routes, and weather conditions. The dataset consists of aggregated journey counts per route per hour provided by the National Transport Authority under an open data licence. No individual passenger records are included and no re-identification is possible from the aggregated data.
