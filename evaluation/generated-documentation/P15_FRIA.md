## Intended Purpose
The research proposal involves analyzing anonymized public transport data to model usage patterns, which poses minimal risks to individuals but may have environmental implications due to computational resource usage. The analysis does not involve personal data, biometric data, or autonomous decision-making, reducing the risk level. However, considerations regarding environmental sustainability, data documentation, and transparency are necessary. The project's impact on social cohesion and democratic processes is minimal, given its focus on transport planning.
