## Broader Impact
The primary ethical risks of this project include potential biases in the NLP pipeline, particularly in the pre-trained transformer models used for classification, which could lead to inaccurate or discriminatory results. Additionally, the project's use of publicly available academic abstracts may still raise concerns about data representativeness and diversity. The overall risk level is considered Low, as the project does not involve personal data, consequential decisions, or deployment affecting individuals. However, the project team should still take steps to mitigate potential biases and ensure the tool's transparency and explainability.

## Potential Misuse
- Bias in NLP pipeline (Low)
- Data representativeness (Low)
- Lack of transparency and explainability (Low)
- Potential misuse of the tool (Low)

## Harm Mitigations
- Conduct thorough analysis of training data for biases and limitations
- Implement transparency and explainability measures in the NLP pipeline
- Consider potential risks of the tool being used in discriminatory or exclusionary ways
- Ensure diversity and representativeness of the target population
