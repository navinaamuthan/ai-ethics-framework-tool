## Project Summary
This project proposes to install camera-based emotion recognition systems in six secondary school classrooms in Dublin to study the relationship between student emotional engagement and learning outcomes. The system will use computer vision to classify student facial expressions into six emotional categories (happy, sad, angry, surprised, fearful, neutral) at 5-second intervals throughout lessons. Data will be collected over one academic term covering approximately 180 students aged 13-17.
