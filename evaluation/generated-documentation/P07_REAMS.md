## Project Summary
This research proposes to apply sentiment analysis and linguistic style detection to a corpus of 5,000 anonymised undergraduate essays submitted to the School of English at Trinity College Dublin. The aim is to investigate whether emotional tone and writing style correlate with academic grade outcomes. Essays will be collected from the past three academic years with student consent obtained retrospectively via email.
