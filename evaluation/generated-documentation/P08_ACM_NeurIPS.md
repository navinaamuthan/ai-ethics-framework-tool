## Broader Impact
The proposed conversational AI chatbot for mental health support triage poses significant ethical risks, including potential misdiagnosis, lack of human oversight, and privacy concerns. The system's reliance on pseudonymised student data and external cloud-based NLP services raises concerns about data protection and potential function creep. Additionally, the chatbot's ability to influence students' decisions about their mental health care may impact their autonomy and human dignity.

## Potential Misuse
- Misdiagnosis or inaccurate assessment (High)
- Lack of human oversight (High)
- Privacy concerns (Medium)
- Function creep (Medium)
- Influence on students' decisions (High)

## Harm Mitigations
- Develop clear guidelines and disclaimers to inform students they are interacting with an AI chatbot
- Conduct user testing to ensure students understand they are interacting with a non-human agent
- Implement robust privacy and data protection measures to prevent breaches of confidentiality
- Develop strategies to prevent the chatbot from being used for deceptive or harmful interactions
- Conduct a thorough assessment of the chatbot's potential impact on democratic processes and social relations
