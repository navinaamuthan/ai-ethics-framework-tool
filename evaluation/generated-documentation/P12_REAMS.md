## Project Summary
This research proposes to develop a machine learning model to optimise hospital bed allocation across three Dublin hospitals. The model will predict patient length of stay, readmission risk, and discharge timing using electronic health records including diagnosis codes, vital signs, medication history, and demographic information including age, gender, and medical card status. The training dataset consists of 80,000 anonymised patient records from 2018-2024. The model will generate daily recommendations for bed managers on patient transfers and early discharge candidates.
