## Project Summary
This research proposes to deploy Amazon Rekognition, a cloud-based facial recognition system, for use by law enforcement agencies across the United States to identify individuals in real-time surveillance footage, body camera recordings, and crime scene images. The system can identify and track up to 100 faces in a single image and match them against databases of tens of millions of faces drawn from arrest records and public sources.
