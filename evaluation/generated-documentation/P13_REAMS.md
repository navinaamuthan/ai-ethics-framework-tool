## Project Summary
This research proposes to conduct a bibliometric analysis of peer-reviewed publications on AI ethics from 2018-2025. The study will use the Scopus and Web of Science databases to identify all publications containing keywords related to AI ethics, responsible AI, algorithmic fairness, and AI governance. The analysis will examine publication trends, citation networks, geographic distribution of research groups, and thematic clustering using topic modelling.
