## Social Impact
The proposed NLP classifier for detecting extremist recruitment content on social media poses significant ethical risks, including the potential for misidentification of legitimate political expression as extremist content, dual-use risks where the classifier could be repurposed for political censorship, and impacts on freedom of expression and non-discrimination. The classifier's ability to process content in multiple languages, including English, Arabic, and Irish, adds complexity to these risks. Furthermore, the lack of engagement with affected communities or civil liberties organisations in the design process exacerbates these concerns. The open-source nature of the tool, while promoting transparency, also raises questions about who might use the tool and for what purposes.
