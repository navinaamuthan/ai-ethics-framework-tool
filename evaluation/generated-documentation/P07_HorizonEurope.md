## Social Impact
The research proposal involves processing personal data, albeit anonymised, and may lead to potential biases in the analysis of academic grade outcomes based on emotional tone and writing style. There is a risk of correlating detectable stylistic differences with students' personal characteristics, such as first language, gender, or socioeconomic background. Additionally, the use of sentiment analysis and linguistic style detection may raise concerns about privacy and potential misuse of the results. The proposal also involves retrospective consent, which may not be sufficient to ensure that students are fully aware of the potential risks and benefits of the research.
