## Social Impact
The research proposal involves sentiment analysis and linguistic style detection on a corpus of anonymised undergraduate essays, which may pose risks related to bias, discrimination, and privacy breach. The use of pseudonymised data and retrospective consent may mitigate some risks, but the potential for harm to students, particularly those from vulnerable groups, remains. The analysis of stylistic differences correlated with students' first language, gender, or socioeconomic background may also raise concerns about fairness and non-discrimination. Furthermore, the potential for biased models and lack of transparency in the NLP pipeline may exacerbate these risks.
