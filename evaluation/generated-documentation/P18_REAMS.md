## Project Summary
This project proposes to develop an autonomous AI research assistant capable of independently searching the web, reading academic papers, summarising findings, and drafting literature review sections. The agent will use a large language model with tool-use capabilities including web browsing, PDF parsing, database querying, and file creation. The agent will operate with minimal human oversight, executing multi-step research tasks overnight and presenting results for review the following morning. ## Mitigations
- Implement robust data protection measures to prevent unauthorized access or processing of personal data
- Develop and implement transparent and explainable AI decision-making processes
- Conduct thorough risk assessments and impact analyses to identify potential negative social impacts
- Establish human oversight and redress mechanisms to ensure that AI system decisions are transparent, accountable, and subject to human review
- Ensure that the AI system is designed and used in ways that respect human rights and dignity
