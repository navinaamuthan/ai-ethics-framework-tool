## Social Impact
The proposed machine learning-based ad delivery optimisation system poses significant ethical risks, including potential discrimination and bias in ad delivery, lack of transparency and explainability, and potential negative impacts on democratic processes and social relations. The system's use of user behavioural, demographic, and interest signals to determine ad delivery may perpetuate existing biases and stereotypes, and the absence of pre-deployment fairness auditing and mechanisms for individual users to understand ad delivery decisions exacerbates these risks. Furthermore, the system's potential to facilitate deceptive interactions, influence political processes, and promote hate speech is a concern. The risks are heightened by the system's potential to impact vulnerable groups, such as minorities and women, and the lack of consideration for universal accessibility and anti-discrimination implications.
