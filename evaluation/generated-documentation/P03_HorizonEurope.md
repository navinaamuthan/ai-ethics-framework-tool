## Social Impact
The proposed deployment of Amazon Rekognition for law enforcement agencies poses significant risks of discrimination, privacy breaches, and physical harm due to its potential for false matches, particularly against people of color. The system's default confidence threshold of 80% has been shown to produce disproportionate false positives, and the lack of independent demographic bias audit and oversight mechanism exacerbates these risks. Furthermore, the use of facial recognition technology raises concerns about surveillance, manipulation, and reputational harm. The risks are heightened by the potential for the technology to be used in consequential decisions, such as criminal investigations, without adequate safeguards.
