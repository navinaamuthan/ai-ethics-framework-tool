## Social Impact
The proposed deployment of Amazon Rekognition for law enforcement agencies poses significant ethical risks, including potential misidentification of individuals, particularly people of color, and the lack of independent oversight mechanisms. The system's default confidence threshold of 80% has been shown to produce false positives, which could lead to wrongful arrests and convictions. Furthermore, the deployment of this technology raises concerns about mass surveillance, privacy, and the potential for biased decision-making. The absence of explicit consent from individuals whose faces are being matched against databases also raises concerns about the processing of special category data.
