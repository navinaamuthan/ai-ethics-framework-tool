## Project Summary
This research proposes to develop an NLP classifier to detect extremist recruitment content on social media platforms including Telegram, Twitter/X, and encrypted messaging forums. The classifier will be trained on a dataset of 20,000 annotated posts collected from known extremist channels alongside 20,000 non-extremist posts covering political activism, religious discussion, and protest organisation.
