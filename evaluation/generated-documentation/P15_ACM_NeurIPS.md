# ACM_NeurIPS ethics documentation — P15
Generated by AIEF from a single harmonised assessment.

## Broader Impact
The research proposal involves analyzing anonymized public transport data to model usage patterns, which poses minimal risks to individuals but may have environmental implications due to computational resource usage. The analysis does not involve personal data, biometric data, or autonomous decision-making, reducing the risk level. However, considerations regarding environmental sustainability, data documentation, and transparency are necessary. The project's impact on social cohesion and democratic processes is minimal, given its focus on transport planning.

## Potential Misuse
- Environmental impact of computational resources
- Data privacy and security
- Lack of transparency in methodology
- Inaccurate forecasting
- Insufficient consideration of social impact

## Harm Mitigations
- Conduct a thorough environmental impact assessment
- Implement transparent and reproducible data analysis methodologies
- Ensure compliance with all relevant data protection regulations
- Regularly review and update the project's ethical considerations
- Explore sustainable computational resources and practices
