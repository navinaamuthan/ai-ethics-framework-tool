## Project Summary
This research proposes to study the relationship between digital workplace behaviours and productivity by monitoring 200 consenting employees at a Dublin technology company over six months. The monitoring system will track application usage, email response times, meeting attendance, keyboard activity patterns, and break frequency. All participants will provide written informed consent and can withdraw at any time without workplace consequences.
