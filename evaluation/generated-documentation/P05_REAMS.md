## Project Summary
This research proposes to deploy a machine learning sepsis prediction algorithm across NHS England hospital trusts to identify patients at risk of sepsis deterioration in emergency department and ward settings. The algorithm uses physiological parameters including respiratory rate, oxygen saturation, temperature, systolic blood pressure, pulse rate, and level of consciousness — as recorded in the National Early Warning Score 2 (NEWS2) framework — alongside laboratory values and electronic health record data, to generate real-time risk scores for clinical staff.
